Introduction

The 3T-523/P-5-T relay is current relay, 5A, provided to circuits to be breaker fail protection. This procedure details the steps required to completely check the proper operation and calibration

 

The first draft initiated by eng. Tareq Ahmed Mohamed, tested at 02 May 03 at Malwy substation 132 Middle Egypt zone and accepted by eng Metawea Hassan Aly: General Director South Menia Networks and approved by Ahmed Saied AbdelAzez: MPIS and QC General Director at ME zone

 Safety Precautions

  • Line isolation is required.

  • Work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses

    •

  • Make sure that CT secondary circuits are shorted before isolating the relay current circuits for current injection

  • Make sure relay contacts are isolated from circuits

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary injection tester

  • Voltmeter, Ammeter, and Test leads 

Work to be Carried Out

Preparations

  1. Make sure CT secondary circuits are shorted at the incoming test blokes before isolating the relay current circuits for test

  2. Look at field maintenance file and compare the position of the setting switches on relay with those shown on the setting sheet

  3. Make sure relay contact was untied before testing

 

Relay test

  1. Switch off the dc "mcb"

  2. Untie terminals to relay contacts from terminal blocks and connect them to stop contact sockets of the tester

  3. Clean the relay contact and inspect tying

  4. Connect current leads from the tester to current terminals of the relay on terminal block as following: Connect single phase current leads from the tester to phase R-R terminals of the relay on the terminal block, and after testing phase R-R connect the current test leads to phase S-S terminals on the terminal block, so after that connect to phase T-T terminals

  5. Test operation value of the current setting of phase R for the relay by increase injected current and record the Iop , and record Ir reset value by decrease injected current

  6. Repeat 4 steps for phase S

  7. Repeat 4 step for phase T

  8. Compare between the results and the setting

  9. Record results in this table
