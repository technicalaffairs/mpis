Introduction

This document details the testing steps P1, which are performed on the fault recorder type 2000 manufacturer Enertic, on a semi annual basis M5 (every 6 months).

Safety Precautions

  • Work order should be issud,

  • The fault recorder should be in service,

  • Appropriate personnel protective equipment must be used.

Tools and Equipment
  • Normal multimeter or digital Multimeter,
  • Complete set of screwdrive.
  • Blower.

Work to be Carried Out

Testing Procedures

Preparation

1.

Clean the outside casing of the fault recorder from the dust using the blower.

2.

Ensure that the operation lamps are in the correct position reference to MPIS doc's no. PC-SS-000- -r0.

3.

Check that all connections of the complete fault recorder system "not loose".

4.

Measure the output of the DC/DC converter to UR2000, and acquisition units.

Testing the Printer

1.

Check the operation of the printer by pressing on the switch no. (5) and switch the power supply of the printer to ON position. To stop the printer, switch the power supply of the printer to OFF position

2.

Check the selection switch of the required printer (SW1, SW2, SW21, SW22, SW23, SW24).

Function Test

1.

Check and review the programming of all functions of the fault recorder.

2.

Check the scales waves form of the sheet o/p and its values.

3.

Function of fault recorder units through the five lines indicating definite values and waveform (computer-Printer-Monitor- Keyboard-Auxiliaries).

4.

Function test must be carried out for master (UR2000) and other units using the keyboard.
