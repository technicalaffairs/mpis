Introduction

This document details the testing steps P1, which are performed on the DFR type 16132, manufacturer Hathaway, on an annually basis Y1 .

Safety Precautions
  • Work order should be issued,
  • The fault recorder should be in service
  • Appropriate personnel protective equipment must be used.

Tools and Equipment
  • Normal or digital multimeter,
  • Complete set of screwdrivers.
  • Blower and musk.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the outside and inside panel of DFR from dust using the blower

  2. Check all parts wiring of DFR system. "not loose"

  3. Check the 4 fuses in the power unit.

  4. Check 32 current shunt.

  5. Check 11 (Ts) Wasting House FT-1 test switch:

    TB (10) the I / P terminal blocks
    Ground Bus.
    Alarm inter face relay (K 1- K 4) fail – operation fault – service)
    G1 + G2 (Alarm relay panel)
    The terminals of to a relay and digital channels to the data acquisition unit (and the of syn-IRIG –printer Ex. Start etc …).

  6. Check the local printer and its functions test.

  7. Check the sensors and transformer of over a reset relay and earth fault relay setting valves (1+2+3(0.8) and 4 90.1)

  8. Check the program of the data acquisition unit and the default valve (Altering DAV par ameters ).

  9. Test and review the calibration to analog charnels on R1to R32 to the tow DAV (from analog and patch Board card ).

  10. Check the A/D (Analog to digital converter) and the fall scrle deflection (4.27) – (gim adjustment).

  11. Check the test function.

  12. At last put the system on auto mode and show the lamps indication.

  13. Doing one rigger and show the result and the rmelo form of the o/p sheit.
