Introduction

The type D115 one system protection operating with switching to the faulty phases is intended for earthen HV networks. It’s substitute type D115 and all variants of types D110 to D114. The protection is suitable for operation in conjunction with out- recessing and coupling of distance protections by means of type DVF 5 and high-frequency carrier equipment.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Line isolation is required
  • Make sure CT's secondary circuits are shunted before isolating the relay current circuits for test current injection.

Tools and Equipment

  • Test sets type KZ 4 & KZ 4 P.
  • Test leads.
  • 380 volt power supply.
  • Screwdriver.
  • Timer.

The Phase Sequence Indicator

  1. After the Supply connection and under normal conditions both signal lamps light equally . When the push button F is pushed one signal lamp lights more brightly L1L2L3 shows correct phase sequence and L1 L2 L3 shows incorrect phase sequence respectively.

  2. The circuit diagram for the operating time measurement is on fig.(1) A mechanical or electronic timer is used for the time measurement. The make contact ( terminals 3-4) serves as the start contact which closes when the push button is pressed and short circuit occurs. Contacts in the tested protection are used to stop the timer.

a) Simulation of single- phase and three phase short circuits:-

Insert fully the selecting plugs VK1 and VK2 into the respective terminals. The current is set with the switch I and the impedance with the switches V I and V2 respectively , and all artificial short circuit is made with the push button K when the switches V1 and V2 are adjusted to a low value ( or to zero ) a short circuit very close to bus-bar is simulated.

b) Simulation of a three – phase short- circuit

After the tests of one – phase or two- phase faults, the protection is perfectly tested . to

Check the sensitivity of direction circuit unit in the protection from the third healthy phase the scheme with the ordinary short- circuit loop of the two phases and small selectable voltage in the previous phase can be used .This test is made with disconnected terminals 5 and 6 . The auxiliary voltage is now connected to the protection from the terminal M and is set with the switch VM. The VM switch scale is valid for calibrating current 2x In .

c) Testing of the starting elements

Starting values of the starting elements are mostly checked at 1 to 3 x In and the full voltage of l OOV. It is useful to disconnect measuring and directional elements of the protection ( e.g. by means of disconnection the auxiliary voltage) during the test. Usually only a rough test of individual phases is sufficient. In any case all starting allows must be the same. For more exact measuring we use a voltmeter and an ammeter connected to the KZ4.

 

 The KZ4 As A Simple Current Or Voltage Supply

The current in KZ4 is adjustable only step by step to 15 A. For fine regulation we connect a resistor of about 20 for 6 to 8 A to the current circuit (between disconnected terminals 7 and 8) . Between the terminals 7 and 8 we can connect also an Ammeter

Voltage tests are possible only for under – voltage relays by lowering the full voltage 11o V or 64 V (against the earth to the operating value of the under – voltage relay . Voltage returns always to 110 V or 64 V. For fine adjustment the current control with the inserted restore between the terminals 7 and 8 is used again.
