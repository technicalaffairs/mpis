Introduction

This Document Lists the Minor Maintenance Procedures P1, which must be performed for 
Becker compressor type SV 250 max pressure/150 discharge on an annual bases as indicated 
by Y1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

Tools and Equipment
  • Personal safety equipment, helmet, and safety glasses.

Work to be Carried Out

It is recommended to change the oil at internals of 2000 operating hours the oil must be changed every 2 years even with less than 2000 hours of overact.

1.

To change oil remover screw plug with oil strainer attached. Let the drain in to a container.

2.

Clean out strainer with solvent. Re-insert straineranc screw plug.

3.

Fill compressor with new approved oil approx. 4 litters of new oil is required.

4.

After compressor with change operation is completed the compressor must be started several times is succession sine the suction to the oil pump was evince during the oil change operation.

5.

Check air filter cartridge, if it is dirty. Blow the cartridge out with compressed air from the inside to outwards. If paper elements is cartridge are damaged in still anew filter cartridge.

6.

Ensure that filter cartridge is properly seated when filter housing is erases.

7.

Check for any annual noises or vibrations.

8.

Check for air leaks is piping and connections.

9.

Read and record gouge pressure and compressor running time.
