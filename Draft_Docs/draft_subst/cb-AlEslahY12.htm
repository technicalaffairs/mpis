Introduction

This document outlines the Major Maintenance procedure P2 , which must be performed every 12 years as indicated  by Y12 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated at Portable Eslah UE zone

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

    •
  • Appropriate personal protective tools must be used, safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses

  • The main and control circuit must be completely disconnected

  • Air pressure of the pneumatic operating mechanism should be discharged from the drain valve of the air reservoir.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, gloves, and shoes,
  • Complete set of tools .
  • Gas detector.

Work to be Carried Out

Inspection of main contacts

Inspection of main contacts, or renewal of ones can be carried out easity from the manhole of the enclosure. The inspection precedure of main contacts is as follows.

  1. Open the manhole cover (301) and loosen the bolts with the hexagon bar spanner

  2. Take out the stationary are contact (214), and the stationary contact holder (215)

  3. Insert the special tool (905) through the hole to take out the nozzle (211)

  4. Insert the another special tool (906) in the same manner to take out the moving are contact (208) and the nozzle (212)

  5. Check the abrasion of the contacts, and renew them if necessary. When the abrasion is not excessive, ammend them with a sand paper of small grain size

  6. Clean the contacts, and apply the special grease over the contact surfaces

  7. Keep the nozzle clear, avoiding any stain

  8. Reassemble the contact and nozzle in the reverse order

  9. At inserting the nozzle, keep screwing until it suddenly becames light to screw

  10. Be sure to clean the inside of the enclosure. When the stationary are contact is not damaged mich at the above inspection (1) and (2), the disassembling of the nozzle and the moving are contact may be omitted

Renewal of Special Desiccant

    It is desirable to renew the sepcial desiccant when the interrupter is inspected. Take care not to leave it for a long time in the atmospheric condition after breaking the packaging of special desiccant. It is recommended that the circuit breaker should be evacuated to vacuum as soon as possible lest the special desiccant should absord too much moisture

Inspection of control valves

    When inspection or renewal of the valve seat and the seal of the control valve is required, the procedure is as follows

  1. Discharge all of the air in the air reservoir
  2. Install the closing lock pin (903) and the tripping lock pin (904) on the pneumatic operating mechanism
  3. Remove the pin (724), and the air pipe
  4. Remove the control valve (703) by loosening the tightening bolts
  5. Inspect the valve seat (717), and renew it if necessary
  6. Loosen the tightening bolt of the cylindrical valve (704) and remove the cylindrical valve (704)
  7. Inspect the seal (719) of the cylindrical valve (704), and renew if necessary
  8. Reassemble the control valve in the reverse order

Inspection of shock absorber

Inspection of the shock absorber is carried out as follows

  1. Check of oil level (refer to fig. 17)
    The check of oil level in the shock absorber is made by using the oil gauge (907) at closed condition
  2. Renewal of oil (refer to fig. 17)
    Exhaust the oil from the oil exhaust point, and charge the new oil to the level of the oil level port. The oil of the following standard should be used
  • Aircraft hydraulic cluid (MIL - N - 5606)
