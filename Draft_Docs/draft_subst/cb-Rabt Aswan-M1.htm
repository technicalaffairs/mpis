Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated  by M1 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated at Rabt Aswan 220kv substation upper Egypt zone ECET.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses.
    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, gloves, and shoes,
  • Complete set of tools

Work to be Carried Out

With the breaker in service, perform the inspections and maintenance operations listed hereunder and record in alogbook anything noteworthy.

  1. Make sure that after every compressor pump-up cycle, the water that might have collected in the compressor condensate tap is automatically and completely dained by the elctrovalve that is connected to the trap bottom

  2. Check the compressor oil level and, if necessary fill up with air compressor oil.

  3. Record the numper of breaker operations and the valuses indicated by the compressor hour meter, if provided

  4. Record the gas pressure values of each pole.
