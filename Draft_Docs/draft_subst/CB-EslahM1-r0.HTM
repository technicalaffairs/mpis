Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on a weekly or  monthly bases as indicated  by W1/M1 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated at Portable Eslah 220kv substation, Upper Egypt zone, ECET.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

    •
  • Appropriate personal protective tools must be used, safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, gloves, and shoes,
  • Complete set of tools

Work to be Carried Out

With the breaker in service, perform the inspections and maintenance operations listed hereunder and record in alogbook anything noteworthy

  1. Check the air gas pressure gauge, approx..5kg/cm2

  2. Check the air pressure gauge.

  3. Check the position indicator.

  4. Check the operating counter.

  5. Inspect the circuit breaker visually.
