Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, to be performed on a daily bases as indicated by D1 in the title block  

The first draft initiated by eng. Fawzy Hanafy Mahmoud at Erooq El-Solb (Solb El Makhsous) substation 220kv West Canal zone. 

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your appropriate personal protective equipment.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes, gas detector

Work to be Carried Out

Note:    The inspection must be Visually Check under the Circuit Breaker being in Service

  1. Check for abnormal noise or odour.

  2. Check for rust or damage at supports or box.

  3. Check that threated bolts and nuts are tightened.

  4. Check for discoloration or heat haze due to overheating of main terminal.

  5. Check that  there is no cracking or flow of porcelain.

Operating Mechanism box, and Control Cabinet

  1. Check the indication of position indicator (to be the same as the service state of the breaker)

  2. Read and record the operation counter.

  3. Check the reading of gas pressure gauge is between marks.

  4. Check the reading of oil pressure gauge is within the range of 320 ~ 340 kg/cm2

  5. Check the oil level.

  6. Check any oil leakage from hydraulic operating mechanism.

  7. Check any gas leakage noise from gas system.
