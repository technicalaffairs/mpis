Introduction

This document lists the tests, which must be performed on all Power transformers as the manufacturer’s recommendations for the frequency of tests.

Safety Precautions
  • A work permit must be issued.
  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes and safety belt when cleaning suspension insulators.

Tools and Equipment

  • Tan d equipment, 5000Volt Megger, Turns Ratio test equipment.
