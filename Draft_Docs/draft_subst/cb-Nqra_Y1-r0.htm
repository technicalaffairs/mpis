Introduction

This document outlines the equipment checklist I1, which must be performed from six months to year as indicated by M6:Y1 in the above titled block.

The first draft initiated at Wadi ELnaqra 220kv substation, upper Egypt zone, ECET.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

    •

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes .... etc
  • SF6 gas detector

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Read the operation counter.

  2. Check visually for the corrosion, paint work, and signs of over heating.

  3. Check operation of permanent heating and condition of air vents

  4. Check the leaks of SF6 gas.

  5. Check the SF6 presser (or SF6 + CF4 gas mixture)

  6. Check the circuit breaker and associated control circuit are working properly, if the apparatus are scarcely used by perform at least 2 C O duty cycles per year.
