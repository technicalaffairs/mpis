Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedures P1, to be performed from three to six years as indicated by Y3:6 in the title block  

The first draft initiated by eng. Kamel Tawfeq Gad Al Sayed, tested at 13 April 2003 by eng. Badry Atia Rofaiel & Kamel Tawfeq Gad Al Sayed, accepted by General Director eng. Moustaf Abd El Nazeer, and approved by Sector Head of  Assuit thermal Power station at UEEPCo.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Carryout the three months maintenance procedures as in MPIS document no. CB-157-r0a

Rust Protection

  1. Remove patches of rust and treat with a rust inhibitor

  2. Make sure there is no oil leakage from dashpot, change dashpot if required and ensure that the new dashpot is filled with oil "S" .

  3. Clean the rust spots and apply new inhibitor with a brush or spray the recommended rust inhibitor is grease "G" or Tectyl 506 (ABB art No. 1241 0011-108) N.B. Tecfyl 506 is a waxy protectine skin ......... wheels

  4. Lubricate the sliding surfaces on the dashpot using Grease "G"

Lubrications

  1. Check that the circuit breaker in open position and the closing spring is off - loaded.

  2. Clean the operating mechanism using vacuum cleaner.

  3. Grease the latches on the operating mechanism using Grease "L"

  4. Grease the shafts in the latch mechanisms using Grease "L"

  5. Check that there is no loose bolts or nuts

Operating Test   ( Closing & opening time measurements)

  1. Check that there is no gas leakage using SF6 gas detector.
