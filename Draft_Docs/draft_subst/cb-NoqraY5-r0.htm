Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procednre P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in  the title block

The first draft initiated at Wadi ELnaqra 220kv substation, Upper Egypt zone, ECET.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes, ...etc , SF6 gas detector.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Read the operation counter

  2. Check visually for the corrosion paint work signs of over heating.

  3. Check operation of permanent heating and the condition of air vents.

  4. Check the leaks of SF6 gas.

  5. Check the SF6 pressure (or SF6 + CF4 gas mixture).

  6. Check SF6 densimeter thresholds. Then adjust pressure to its rated value.

  7. Check arcing finger wear.

  8. Check tightening of parts not subjected to pressure (frame - deck - cubicle).

  9. Check tightening of low voltage terminals.

  10. Check relay operation.

  11. Check latch operation.

  12. Measure operating times for poles and auxiliary contacts.

  13. Carry out operations at recommended duties and rated voltage.
