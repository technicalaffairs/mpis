Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every five years or every 500 operation as indicated  by Y5 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated at Rabt Aswan 220kv substation, upper Egypt zone, ECET

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Appropriate personal protective equpment must be used, safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses

  • Circuit breaker must be fully isolated

  • Earthing switch must be closed on both sides of the breaker

  • The circuit breaker should be switched off and ground the CB an both sides.

  • Switch off control current for CB (d.c)

  • Switch on the ventilation   

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety glove, breathing, helmet, shoes

  • Complete set of maintenance hand tools

  • Portable pressure gauges, Emergency light, Blower

  • Timing equipment, SF6 Gas detector

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Carryout the annual inspection as stated in the MPIS document # CB-125-r0

  2. Check any gas leaks using SF6 gas detector,

  3. With the control circuit at its rated voltage and SF6 air systems at their respective rated pressures, perform same closing and opening operations, recording the operation times with a multichannel oscillograph

  4. If the time value don't corresponding to those indicated in the factory certificates, the filters, orifices and control valves of the operating mechanisms that show abnormal values shall be cleaned out, inspected and reassembled.

  5. Measure the operating tims again,

  6. Take an SF6sample from each breaker pole and measure its humidity.

  7. If the gas moisture in one pole is higher than 250 PPMv, the SF6 contained in it must be reconditioned and the molecular sieves replaced.
