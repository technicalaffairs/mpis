Safety Precautions

  • The qualified operation staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  •  safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses

Tools and Equipment

  • No special tools

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check visualy for abnormal noise or odour

  2. Check visualy for rust or damage at supports or box

  3. Check that threated bolts and nuts are tightned

  4. Ensure the indication of position indicator as the same as the service state of the breaker

  5. Check and record operation the operation counter

  6. Check the indication of gas pressure gauge reference value between 6.5 N 7.5 bar

  7. Check the indication of oil pressure gauge reference value between 315 N 335 bar

  8. Check the oil level gauge accordance with minimum and maximum level .

  9. Check the leakage of gas system

  10. Check the leakage of oil from hydraulic operating mechanism
