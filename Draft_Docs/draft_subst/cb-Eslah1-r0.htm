Introduction

This document outlines the Minor Maintenance procedure P1 , which must be performed every 6 year bases as indicated  by Y6 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated at Portable Eslah UE zone

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

    •
  • Appropriate personal protective tools must be used, safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses

  • The main and control circuit must be completely disconnected

  • Air pressure of the pneumatic operating mechanism should be discharged from the drain valve of the air reservoir.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, gloves, and shoes,
  • Complete set of tools .
  • Gas detector.

Work to be Carried Out

The main and control circuit must be completely disconnected and air pressure of the pneumatic operating mechanism should be discharged from the drain valve of the air reservoir.

  1. Check the setting of gas pressure switch.

  2. Check the setting of  air pressure switch.

  3. Check the control wiring for loose connection.

  4. Check the another bolts for loosening..

  5. Check the auxiliary switch contacts.

  6. Check the mechanical linkage for loose connection.

  7. check pipe fittings and tighten pipe connections.

  8. Check the gas leakage.

  9. Check the pneumatic operating mechanism.
